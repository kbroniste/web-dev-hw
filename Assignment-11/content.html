The Giant Panda

Information you never knew






Giant panda's diets mostly include bamboo, sometimes birds or rodents. A typical panda eats half the day. It takes about 28 pounds of bamboo to fulfill the intake needs of a giant panda. Panda's have elongated wrist bones that function like thumbs.

Pandas in the wild live only in remote, mountainous regions in central China. These bamboo forests remain cool and wet. Pandas can climb as high as 13,000 feet during the summer season to feed on higher slopes.
