Meet The Pack

This is a brief interduction to my little pack. I firmly believe that my pups were destined for me and found me at my darkest times. They have stood by my side through many trials and helped keep me going.




Rocky AKA "Rocky Dog/ Mister Mister"





Rocky is a 10 year old Australian Sheppart/Black Lab mix. He joined the pack back in 2017. Rocky enjoys car rides and rolling in the grass on sunny days. He absolutley hates water and rain. However, he can be found burying his head in the snow on a nice day. Rocky enjoys the simple things in life, mostly frequent naps cuddled up to his favorite humans. Rocky gets to spend his days enjoying his golden years with frequent treats, belly rubs, and snuggles. He loves to sleep in the recliner in the living room. But his favorite spot is in bed, underneath all the covers, with his girl. He even has his own pillow.

Mowgli AKA "Mo Bear"




Mowgli will be 1 year at the end of May. He is a husky hybrid. He loves to play in water, unlike his brother. He loves to play with his best friend Stella. Mowgli will try and play with Rocky Dog, but Rocky prefers to spend his days napping away in a comfortable chair. Mowgli's favorite game is distract Mom while she is trying to do her homework during the week. He has learned that by doing this his Kong toy gets filled with his favorite treat, peanut butter.
